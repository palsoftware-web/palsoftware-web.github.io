Pastiera is an Android input method for devices with a physical keyboard, such as the Unihertz Titan 2 and Q25. It is built around faster hardware typing, configurable modifier behavior, flexible keyboard layouts, SYM and variation layers, suggestions, shortcuts, and backup.

Use these pages when you want to understand the app itself:
